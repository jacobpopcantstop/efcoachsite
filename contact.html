Quick Questions

Do you offer a trial session?

Yes—your first paid session is a "trial" at the regular rate. If it's not a fit, there's no obligation to continue.

How soon can we start?

Availability varies, but I can usually schedule an initial session within 1-2 weeks of our consultation call.

Do you work with schools?

I can communicate with teachers and learning specialists as needed (with your permission). For IEP meetings, that's a separate service.

What if my child doesn't want coaching?

This is common! Part of what I do is build buy-in with reluctant students. Most warm up quickly once they realize this isn't "more tutoring."
